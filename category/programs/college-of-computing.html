Overview

The College of Computing is dedicated to providing students with a strong foundation in computer science and information technology. Our programs are designed to equip graduates with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the rapidly evolving tech industry. With experienced faculty and modern facilities, we prepare students to meet the challenges of the digital world.

Program Offerings:

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS)

The BSCS program focuses on computational theories, algorithm design, and software development. Graduates of this program will be well-versed in problem-solving, programming, and system architecture, making them highly competitive in various IT and software development roles.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

The BSIT program provides students with practical knowledge in system administration, networking, cybersecurity, and IT solutions development. Graduates will have the necessary technical skills to manage and improve technological infrastructures in various industries.

Admission Requirements

  • Original Grade 12 Senior High School Report Card (F-138)
  • Original F137
  • Original Certificate of Good Moral Character issued in the current year
  • Two (2) identical copies of recent 2”x2” studio photo (white background)
  • Clear photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Clear photocopy of Grade 12 Senior High School Diploma
