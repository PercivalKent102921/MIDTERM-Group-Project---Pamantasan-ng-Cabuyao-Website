Requirements

The following requirements must be submitted to the Registrar's Office before admission to any academic program:

Grade 11
  • Original Form 138 / SF9-JHS (Progress Report Card)
  • Original Form 137 / SF10-JHS (Academic Record)
  • PSA-issued Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character or School Recommendation
  • Medical Certificate

Freshmen
  • Original Form 138 / SF9-SHS
  • Original Form 137 / SF10-SHS
  • PSA-issued Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character or School Recommendation
  • Medical Certificate
Transferees
  • Certificate of Transfer (Honorable Dismissal)
  • Official Transcript of Records
  • PSA-issued Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character or Dean/Program Head Recommendation
  • Medical Certificate
Online Pre-Registration
  1. Visit the application portal: admission.pnc.edu.ph
  2. Ensure your email is active. All admission communication will be via email, including a confirmation of submission.
  3. Online registration closes on April 7, 2025.
Onsite

Please review safety protocols at pnc.edu.ph/campus-safety before visiting.

  1. Visit the PNC campus during office hours.
  2. Submit initial requirements from Jan 27 to Apr 7, 2025.
  3. Only pre-registered applicants who submit documents during this period will be scheduled for assessment exams.
  4. Submit complete admission requirements before classes start.
