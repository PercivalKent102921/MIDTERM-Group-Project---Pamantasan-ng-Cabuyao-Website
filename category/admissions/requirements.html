Requirements

University of Cabuyao

Different Program Requirements

"Dangal ng Bayan" - Bringing pride and honor to the nation.

Basic Education Department (Preschool, Grade School, and Junior High School) +
  • Report Card – Original and Photocopy
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Birth Certificate: Original and Photocopy
  • Baptismal Certificate (if Catholic): Original and Photocopy
  • Letters of Certification and Recommendation
  • Four (4) pieces of 2”x2” studio photo (white background)
  • Elementary Diploma for Junior High School Applicants
Senior High School +
  • Learner’s Progress Report Card (SF9)
  • Learner’s Permanent Academic Record (SF10)
  • Certificate of Junior High School Completion
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Two (2) identical copies of 2”x2” studio photo
College and Dentistry Freshmen +
  • Original Grade 12 Senior High School Report Card
  • Original F137 (with request letter from OLFU)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Two (2) copies of 2”x2” studio photo
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • For married female applicants: Marriage Certificate photocopy
  • Photocopy of Grade 12 Diploma
